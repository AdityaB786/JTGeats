JTGeats - Authentic Home Food Delivery
JTGeats is a modern, responsive landing page for a food delivery service specializing in authentic, home-cooked Indian meals. The platform connects customers craving homemade flavors with local home kitchens.

Responsive Navigation: A mobile-friendly navbar with a toggle menu.

Hero Section: High-impact introduction with a search functionality to find specific dishes.

Home Kitchen Grid: Dynamic display of local kitchens (populated via JavaScript).

Popular Items Carousel: An interactive slider powered by Swiper.js to showcase trending dishes.

Dish Request System: A built-in modal form allowing users to request specific dishes not currently on the menu.

Video Integration: A dedicated service section featuring a custom-styled video player.

Contact & Seller Lead Form: A clean, functional form for general inquiries or prospective sellers.
